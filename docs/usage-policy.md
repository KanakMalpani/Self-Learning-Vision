# Usage Policy

- Use Self-Learning Vision as a personal memory assistant for people you are allowed to remember from your own photos.
- Respect local laws and biometric data requirements; obtain consent where required.
- Do not use Self-Learning Vision for surveillance, stalking, harassment, or discriminatory use cases.
- Do not use it for covert identification or to deanonymize people without explicit permission.
- Do not use recognition output for decisions affecting rights, access, employment, housing, credit, safety, or similar high-impact areas.
- Keep outputs confidential; redact sensitive data before sharing logs, screenshots, demos, or reports.
- Disable or audit any future paid, third-party, public-web, or public-figure providers before enabling them.
